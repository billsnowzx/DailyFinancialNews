# Daily 8am SGT Report Spec

## Operating Window

- Delivery target: 8:00am SGT.
- Final content cutoff: 7:00am SGT.
- Final ingestion pass: 6:30am SGT.
- Context: 8:00am SGT equals 8:00pm ET on the prior calendar day, roughly US cash close plus four hours.

## Report Template

### TL;DR

Five bullets, maximum 60 words total. Each bullet should connect a market move to a plausible driver.

### Cross-Asset Snapshot

Levels and percentage changes for the configured instrument universe:

- Equities: S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, STOXX Europe 600, Nikkei 225, Hang Seng Index, CSI 300.
- Rates: US 2Y, US 10Y, US 30Y, Bund 10Y, JGB 10Y.
- FX: DXY, EURUSD, USDJPY, USDCNH, AUDUSD.
- Commodities: WTI, Brent, gold, copper, iron ore.
- Crypto: BTC.

### By Asset Class

Equities, rates, FX, and commodities each get three to five sentences tying moves to drivers.

### Narrative Drivers

Two to four themes. Each theme needs supporting evidence and source links. Single-sourced claims must be labeled.

### Social Pulse

Separate from headline news. Summarize what Substack, Reddit, and eventually X / 小红书 are talking about. Label this as sentiment, not verified fact.

### Day Ahead

Economic data, central-bank speakers, earnings, and auctions in the next 24 hours.

## Quality Rules

- Numbers in prose must match the market snapshot table.
- Claims need source attribution.
- Social claims must not be presented as factual confirmation.
- If there is insufficient evidence for a section, say so explicitly rather than inventing detail.
